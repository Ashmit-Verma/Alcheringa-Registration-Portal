Guidelines
  1. 1. Complete your profile by filling all the details in the Profile" tab.
  2. 2. Add members to your team in the "Members" tab, if required.
  3. 3. Now, click on to Competitions" tab to register for the events.
  4. 4. Select your particular event and hit 'Register button.
  5. 5. Select the individuals participating in that event.
  6. 6. In events, it will prompt you to provide the link of your performance. In that case, upload the video file on your Google drive and paste the sharing link.
